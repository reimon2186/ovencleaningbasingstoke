Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote customized to your needs. After you approve the estimate, we’ll schedule an appointment at a time that suits you best. On the day of your booking, we’ll give you a quick call before we arrive, ensuring a smooth and efficient visit.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

When we arrive, we begin by examining your oven for any potential issues and confirming that all components are functioning properly. We then protect your kitchen floors and counters with drop cloths or other coverings, preventing any mess or damage during the cleaning process.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We remove all detachable parts—racks, trays, and panels—and place them in our heated dip tank located in the van. This soak softens and breaks down built-up grime. Meanwhile, the interior of your oven is treated with eco-friendly products to remove burnt-on carbon and restore its sheen.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

After thoroughly cleaning the interior, we polish every surface to achieve a pristine, just-like-new appearance. We then meticulously reassemble all the components, making sure they are secure and fully operational. Our high standards ensure a flawless result each and every time.

Step 5: Final Touches

Once the cleaning is complete, we conduct a final inspection to ensure your oven is spotless and running optimally. We also tidy up the work area, returning your kitchen to its original state—except, of course, for your gleaming, refreshed oven that’s ready to use right away.
