Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

Begin by reaching out for a complimentary, no-obligation quote, customized to your oven’s condition. Once you’re satisfied, we’ll find a service slot that matches your availability. On the day of your appointment, you’ll receive a brief call or text confirming we’re en route.

Step 2: Inspection & Precautions

On arrival, our technicians conduct a thorough inspection of your oven, noting any existing issues or areas that need extra attention. We also protect your kitchen floors and countertops with coverings to ensure minimal mess or risk of damage.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We remove all detachable parts—racks, trays, and panels—and immerse them in our heated dip tank, which efficiently breaks down baked-on grease. Meanwhile, we apply eco-friendly cleaning agents to the oven’s interior, loosening accumulated carbon deposits and restoring a fresh, gleaming surface.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

After each component is cleaned, rinsed, and polished, we carefully reassemble your oven, ensuring every piece is aligned for optimal performance. Our close attention to detail guarantees a flawless finish and proper functionality.

Step 5: Final Touches

We finish with a comprehensive inspection, verifying your oven is perfectly clean and operating correctly. Before leaving, we tidy up our workspace, leaving your kitchen exactly as we found it—except for your renewed oven that’s ready for immediate use.
