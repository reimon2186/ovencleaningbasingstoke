Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

Your journey begins by contacting us for a free, no-obligation quote customized to your oven’s condition. If you’re happy with the estimate, we’ll schedule a convenient service time. Expect a quick courtesy call on the day of your appointment, keeping you fully informed of our arrival.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

After we arrive, we carry out a thorough inspection of your oven to identify any problem areas. To protect your kitchen, we cover your floors and countertops, ensuring no mess or damage occurs while we work.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We remove all detachable parts—like racks, trays, and panels—and soak them in our heated dip tank located in the van. This process loosens grease and stubborn grime. Meanwhile, we apply eco-friendly cleaning agents to your oven’s interior to dissolve built-up carbon and restore its original shine.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

Once each part is scrubbed clean and polished, we reassemble your oven with precision, confirming every component is correctly positioned. Our focused attention to detail ensures a spotless, near-new finish and reliable performance.

Step 5: Final Touches

To conclude, we carry out a final inspection to confirm your oven meets our high standards of cleanliness and functionality. We also tidy up our workspace, leaving your kitchen as it was—except with a rejuvenated, sparkling oven ready for your next culinary creation.
