Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

Start by contacting us for a free, no-obligation quote that’s tailored to your oven’s condition. Once you’re happy with the estimate, we’ll find a service slot that fits your calendar. Expect a short reminder call on the day of your appointment, letting you know when we’ll arrive.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

Upon arrival, we thoroughly inspect your oven, pinpointing any issues or particularly grimy spots. We then protect your floors and countertops with coverings, ensuring your kitchen stays clean and safeguarded while we work.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We remove any detachable parts—like racks, trays, and panels—and soak them in our specialized heated dip tank in the van. Meanwhile, eco-friendly solutions are applied to your oven’s interior, dissolving grease and baked-on residue to reveal a bright, refreshed surface.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

Once each part is fully cleaned, rinsed, and polished, we carefully put your oven back together, making sure everything is positioned correctly. Our attention to detail ensures that your oven both looks impeccable and functions smoothly after the cleaning.

Step 5: Final Checks & Tidy-Up

We finish by giving your oven one last inspection to confirm it meets our high standards of cleanliness and performance. We then remove our protective materials, leaving your kitchen just as tidy as we found it—except with a gleaming oven ready for action.
