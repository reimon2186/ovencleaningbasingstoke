Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking & Convenience

It all starts when you contact us for a free, no-obligation quote. Once you agree, we schedule an appointment at a time that fits your calendar. Expect a courtesy call on the day of service to confirm our arrival, so you’re never caught off guard.

Step 2: Inspection & Protection

We begin by inspecting your oven, noting any existing issues or special concerns. We then protect your kitchen surfaces and floors with coverings, ensuring no mess or damage occurs while we work our magic.

Step 3: Deep-Cleaning & Degreasing

We remove every detachable component—such as racks, trays, and back panels—and place them in a heated dip tank inside our van, where they soak to loosen hardened grease. Meanwhile, we treat your oven’s interior with eco-friendly products that help eliminate burnt-on residue, restoring its original shine.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

After cleaning and rinsing each component, we polish them to ensure a sleek finish. Then, we methodically reassemble your oven, making sure every piece is aligned correctly for optimal performance. Our close attention to detail guarantees a spotless result every time.

Step 5: Final Inspection & Cleanup

Once the oven is reassembled, we perform a last quality check to confirm it looks and functions perfectly. We also tidy up our workspace, returning your kitchen to its original state—only now, you have a pristine oven ready for your next cooking adventure.
