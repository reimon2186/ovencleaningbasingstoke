Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Easy Booking & Confirmation

Kick things off by contacting us for a free, no-obligation estimate tailored to your oven’s needs. After you approve, we’ll secure a convenient time slot for your appointment. On the day of service, we’ll give you a courtesy call so you know we’re on our way.

Step 2: Inspection & Prep Work

Upon arrival, our team conducts a comprehensive check of your oven, noting any problem areas and confirming all components are operational. To protect your home, we place covers on your kitchen floors and counters, preventing any accidental spills or scratches.

Step 3: Intensive Cleaning & Degreasing

We remove all detachable parts—like trays, racks, and panels—and immerse them in our heated dip tank in the van. This soaking process efficiently loosens built-up grease and residues. Meanwhile, the oven’s interior is treated with eco-friendly solutions that dissolve stubborn grime, leaving a refreshed shine behind.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

After soaking and cleaning, each element is rinsed and polished to a glossy finish. We then reassemble the oven with care, making sure everything aligns perfectly for optimal functionality. Our thorough approach ensures your oven looks and operates like new.

Step 5: Final Check & Cleanup

To wrap up, we carry out a final review, confirming your oven meets our high cleanliness and performance standards. We also tidy up our workspace, leaving your kitchen as it was—except now you have a gleaming oven ready for immediate use.
