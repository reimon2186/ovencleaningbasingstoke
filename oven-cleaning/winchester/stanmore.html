Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

Your journey begins with a free, no-obligation quote customized to your oven’s condition. After you approve the estimate, we’ll arrange a service appointment at a time that suits your routine. Expect a brief courtesy call on the day, so you know exactly when we’ll arrive.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

Upon arrival, our technicians carry out a detailed inspection of your oven, identifying any issues or heavily soiled areas. We also protect your floors and countertops with coverings, ensuring your kitchen remains clean and unharmed throughout the process.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

Next, we remove every detachable piece—such as trays, racks, and panels—and submerge them in our specially heated dip tank located in the van. This soaking process dissolves caked-on grease, while we treat your oven’s interior with eco-friendly solutions that break down burnt-on residue, reviving its sleek appearance.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

After the parts have been cleaned, rinsed, and polished, we meticulously reassemble your oven, confirming each element is correctly aligned for optimal operation. Our careful attention to detail ensures your oven looks like new and functions reliably once we’re finished.

Step 5: Final Touches

We conclude with a thorough inspection to verify your oven meets our strict standards of cleanliness and performance. Lastly, we tidy up our workspace, leaving your kitchen in the same condition we found it—apart from your radiant oven, ready for immediate use.
