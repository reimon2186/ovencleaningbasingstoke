Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Scheduling & Prep

Your oven’s transformation begins when you contact us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your specific requirements. Once you approve the estimate, we’ll book an appointment at a time convenient for you. On the day of service, expect a quick call so you’ll know exactly when we’ll arrive.

Step 2: Inspection & Safeguarding

After we arrive, we thoroughly inspect your oven, noting any problematic areas or worn components. We then cover your kitchen floors and nearby surfaces with protective materials, keeping your space clean and free from accidental damage while we work.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning & Degreasing

We remove all detachable elements—racks, trays, panels—and place them in a heated dip tank in the van, where they soak to loosen tough grease. Meanwhile, we treat your oven’s interior with eco-friendly solutions, dissolving carbon buildup and restoring a polished shine.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

When the parts are cleaned, rinsed, and polished, we carefully put your oven back together, ensuring each piece is placed correctly for optimal functionality. Our attention to detail guarantees a sleek finish and a fully operational appliance.

Step 5: Final Review & Cleanup

Lastly, we conduct a final inspection to confirm your oven meets our strict standards of cleanliness and performance. We also tidy up our workspace, leaving your kitchen looking just as it did before—except with a newly revitalized oven ready for your next cooking session.
