Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

Your journey starts by getting in touch for a complimentary, no-obligation quote based on your oven’s condition. If you’re satisfied with our estimate, we’ll book a convenient appointment. On the day of service, we’ll give you a short courtesy call, ensuring you know when we’ll arrive.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

Upon arrival, our technicians perform a thorough inspection of your oven to spot any issues or hard-to-reach areas. We then protect your floors and countertops with coverings, keeping your kitchen free from mess or damage during the cleaning process.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We remove all detachable parts—racks, trays, and panels—and place them in our heated dip tank, which gently dissolves baked-on grease. Meanwhile, we apply eco-friendly solutions to the oven’s interior, lifting burnt-on residue and restoring a bright, refreshed surface.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

Once every component is thoroughly cleaned and rinsed, we polish them to enhance their shine. We then carefully reassemble your oven, ensuring each part is properly placed for optimal operation. Our attention to detail yields a finished product that both looks and works flawlessly.

Step 5: Final Touches

Finally, we conduct a last inspection to confirm that your oven meets our stringent standards of cleanliness and performance. We also tidy up our work area, leaving your kitchen exactly as we found it—except for your renewed, gleaming oven ready for immediate use.
