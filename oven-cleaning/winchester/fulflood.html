Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking & Convenience

Begin by getting in touch for a free, no-obligation quote—tailored to your specific oven and level of grime. Once you agree, we’ll book a time slot that aligns with your routine. On the day of the service, expect a brief call to let you know we’re on our way.

Step 2: Inspection & Precaution

After we arrive, we start with a thorough inspection, identifying any problem areas and ensuring that all oven components are working properly. We also protect your floors and countertops with coverings to keep your kitchen clean and damage-free as we work.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning & Degreasing

We detach all removable elements—racks, trays, panels—and submerge them in our heated dip tank, where even the most stubborn grease and residue will soften and lift off. Meanwhile, inside the oven, we use eco-friendly solutions to break down carbon build-up and restore a fresh shine.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

Once every piece has been thoroughly cleaned and rinsed, we polish them for a renewed gleam. We then carefully reassemble your oven, making certain each component is properly secured. Our high standards guarantee a flawless finish and reliable performance..

Step 5: Final Verification & Cleanup

Lastly, we perform a comprehensive inspection to confirm your oven is both spotless and functioning optimally. We then remove our protective coverings, leaving your kitchen precisely as we found it—except for your oven, which will look and work better than ever.
