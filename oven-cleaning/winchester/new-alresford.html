Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Scheduling & Coordination

Start by contacting us for a free, no-obligation quote that’s customized to your oven’s condition. Once you’re satisfied, we’ll schedule a convenient appointment. Expect a brief courtesy call on the day, confirming our arrival so you’re never caught off guard.

Step 2: Inspection & Protection

Upon arrival, we conduct a thorough assessment of your oven, ensuring we’re aware of any specific areas needing attention. We also use floor and countertop coverings to protect your kitchen from accidental spills or damage during the cleaning process.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

All detachable parts—such as racks, trays, and panels—are removed and immersed in a heated dip tank in our van, where even the most stubborn grease is loosened. Meanwhile, we treat the oven’s interior with eco-friendly solutions to dissolve baked-on residue, revealing a clean, polished finish.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

After each component has been thoroughly scrubbed, rinsed, and polished, we carefully reassemble your oven, making sure every piece fits correctly. Our meticulous approach guarantees a near-new shine and ensures that your oven functions at its full potential.

Step 5: Final Inspection & Cleanup

Before we conclude, we carry out a final inspection to confirm that your oven meets our high standards of cleanliness and performance. We tidy up our work area, leaving your kitchen exactly as we found it—apart from your revitalized oven, ready for immediate use.
