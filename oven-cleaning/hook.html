Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

RReach out to us for a free, no-obligation quote that’s tailored to your needs. If you’re happy with the estimate, we’ll schedule an appointment at a time that works best for you. On the day of the service, we’ll call ahead to confirm our arrival, ensuring you’re prepared and never caught off guard.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

Once we arrive, we’ll carefully inspect your oven for any existing issues and verify that everything is functioning correctly. Before we start, we take precautions to protect your floors and kitchen surfaces, using coverings and mats to ensure no mess or damage occurs during the cleaning process.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We disassemble all removable parts—including racks, trays, and panels—and place them into our heated dip tank in the van. This soak helps dissolve stubborn grime and grease. Meanwhile, we concentrate on the oven’s interior, using our eco-friendly solutions to remove burnt-on carbon and restore a sparkling shine.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

After thoroughly cleaning the interior, we polish every surface for a sleek, like-new appearance. We then reassemble each part of your oven with meticulous care, ensuring everything is secure and in optimal working order. Our attention to detail guarantees consistently flawless outcomes.

Step 5: Final Touches

We finish up by performing a last, detailed inspection of your oven, confirming that it’s spotless and fully functional. We also clean up our work area, leaving your kitchen exactly as we found it—except with a gleaming oven ready for action.
