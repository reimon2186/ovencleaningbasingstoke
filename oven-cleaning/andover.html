Our Comprehensive Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

Getting started is simple. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your needs. Once confirmed, we’ll book a time that works best for you. On the day of your service, our team will give you a courtesy call to let you know we’re on the way.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

Upon arrival, we inspect your oven to identify specific issues and ensure all components are functioning properly. We also protect your kitchen floors and countertops with mats and coverings, ensuring a mess-free experience.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We disassemble all removable parts, such as racks, trays, and panels, and place them in our heated dip tank located in the van. This process dissolves tough grease and grime. Meanwhile, we use specialized tools and eco-friendly solutions to clean the oven’s interior, removing carbon buildup and restoring its shine.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

After deep cleaning, we polish every surface to give your oven a sleek, like-new appearance. Once all components are cleaned and dried, we carefully reassemble the oven, ensuring it’s ready for use.

Step 5: Final Touches

Before leaving, we perform a final inspection to ensure your oven is spotless and functioning at its best. We also tidy up the workspace, leaving your kitchen as clean as we found it.
