Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

Begin by contacting us for a free, no-obligation quote, tailored to your specific needs. Once you’re happy with our estimate, we’ll book a convenient appointment. On the day, you’ll receive a quick courtesy call, so you’ll know exactly when we’re set to arrive.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

When we arrive, we begin with a thorough inspection of your oven, noting any areas needing particular attention. We also protect your floors and countertops with coverings, ensuring that no spills or debris affect your kitchen while we work.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We carefully disassemble all removable parts, including trays, racks, and back panels, and transfer them to our heated dip tank located in the van. While these components soak to remove stubborn grease and grime, we focus on the oven interior, using eco-friendly solutions to eliminate carbon buildup and restore its original shine.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

Once the interior is thoroughly cleaned, we polish all surfaces for a sleek, like-new finish. We then reassemble the oven, ensuring each component is securely in place. Our team pays close attention to detail to guarantee a flawless result, leaving no area untouched.

Step 5: Final Touches

After reassembling your oven, we perform a final inspection to ensure everything is spotless and functioning correctly. We tidy up the workspace, leaving your kitchen as clean as we found it. With your oven looking immaculate and ready for use, you can enjoy cooking with a renewed sense of ease and confidence.
