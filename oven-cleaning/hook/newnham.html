Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

To begin, get in touch for a free, no-obligation quote customized to your oven’s specific condition. Once you’re happy with our estimate, we’ll pencil you in at a time that suits your routine. Expect a courtesy call on the day of service so you know exactly when to anticipate our arrival.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

After we arrive, we examine your oven thoroughly, pinpointing any issues and noting any parts that need extra care. We also protect your floors and countertops with coverings, ensuring your kitchen remains clean and undamaged while we work.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We remove any detachable parts—racks, trays, and panels—and place them in our heated dip tank in the van, where tough grease is softened and lifted away. Meanwhile, we apply eco-friendly products to your oven’s interior, dissolving burnt-on grime and reviving its original finish.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

Once the components have soaked, we scrub, rinse, and polish them to a gleaming finish. We then reassemble your oven with precision, confirming everything is fitted correctly for seamless functionality. Our attention to detail ensures a spotless result and reliable performance.

Step 5: Final Touches

Before concluding, we carry out a final inspection to verify your oven meets our high standards of cleanliness and operation. Finally, we tidy up our workspace, leaving your kitchen exactly as we found it—except now you have a fresh, renewed oven ready for all your cooking adventures.
