Our Comprehensive Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Appointment

Getting started is easy—contact us at 07592 463745 or through our online booking form. We’ll provide a free, no-obligation quote and schedule a time that fits your schedule. On the day of your appointment, we’ll give you a courtesy call to confirm our arrival.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

Upon arrival, our team inspects your oven to identify any issues and ensure all components are functioning correctly. We take care to protect your kitchen with mats and coverings, so there’s no mess during the process.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We disassemble all removable parts, including racks, trays, and panels, and soak them in our heated dip tank to dissolve stubborn grease and grime. Meanwhile, we clean the oven’s interior using specialized tools and eco-friendly solutions.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

After a thorough clean, we polish every surface, reassemble the oven, and ensure everything is securely in place. This attention to detail guarantees a like-new finish.

Step 5: Final Touches

Finally, we tidy up and leave your kitchen as we found it—only now with a spotless, revitalized oven ready to use.
