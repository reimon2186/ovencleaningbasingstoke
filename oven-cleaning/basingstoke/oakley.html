Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

Start by contacting us for a free, no-obligation quote based on your specific needs. After you approve, we’ll set a service date and time that suits your schedule. Expect a quick heads-up call on the day of the appointment, so you know exactly when we’ll arrive.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

Once on-site, we carry out an in-depth inspection of your oven, checking for any potential issues or areas that require extra care. We also protect your floors and countertops with coverings to prevent any accidental mess or damage during the cleaning process.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

All detachable components—such as racks, trays, and panels—are removed and placed in our heated dip tank in the van. This immersion effectively dissolves the toughest grease. Meanwhile, we apply eco-friendly cleaning agents to the oven’s interior to break down carbon buildup and restore its original shine.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

After thoroughly cleaning and polishing each part, we carefully reassemble your oven, ensuring every piece fits perfectly and functions correctly. Our methodical approach guarantees an oven that not only looks brand new but also operates smoothly.

Step 5: Final Touches

To wrap up, we perform a final examination to confirm the oven is spotless and functioning optimally. We then clear away our workspace, leaving your kitchen just the way we found it—except with a brilliantly restored oven at the center.
