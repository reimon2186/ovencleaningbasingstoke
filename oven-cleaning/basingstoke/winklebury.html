Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

It all begins when you contact us for a free, no-obligation quote customized to your specific needs. Once you approve, we’ll set a date and time that best fits your schedule. On the day of your appointment, we’ll provide a courteous call so you’ll know exactly when to expect us.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

Upon arrival, we conduct a comprehensive examination of your oven, identifying any potential issues and verifying that all components function correctly. We also safeguard your kitchen floors and countertops with protective materials, ensuring no mess or damage occurs while we work.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We remove all detachable components—such as racks, trays, and panels—and place them in our specialized heated dip tank to dissolve baked-on grease. Meanwhile, we apply eco-friendly agents to the oven’s interior, eliminating stubborn grime and restoring its sleek appearance.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

After thoroughly cleaning and drying each component, we polish them for a renewed, gleaming look. Then, we carefully reassemble your oven, ensuring every part is properly aligned and secure. Our attention to detail helps guarantee flawless operation once we’re finished.

Step 5: Final Touches

To finish, we perform a final inspection to make sure your oven meets our exacting standards of cleanliness and functionality. We also tidy up our workspace, leaving your kitchen as we found it—except with a beautifully refreshed oven ready for action.
