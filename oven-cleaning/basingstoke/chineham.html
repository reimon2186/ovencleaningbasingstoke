Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

Start by reaching out for a free, no-obligation quote customized to your oven’s specifics. Once you’re happy with our estimate, we’ll pick a convenient day and time to visit. On the day of service, expect a quick call or message, so you know exactly when we’ll arrive.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

After we arrive, we conduct a thorough inspection of your oven, identifying any problem areas or worn components. We also protect your kitchen surfaces with drop cloths or coverings, ensuring no mess or damage while we work.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We remove all detachable parts—racks, trays, panels—and submerge them in our heated dip tank in the van. This process dissolves even the toughest grime. Meanwhile, we apply eco-friendly cleaning agents inside your oven to break down burnt-on residue, restoring its original sheen.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

Once every piece has been cleaned, rinsed, and dried, we polish each surface to a near-new shine. Then, we reassemble your oven with care, making certain all parts fit precisely for optimal operation. Our diligent approach ensures your oven looks immaculate and functions perfectly.

Step 5: Final Touches

Before we wrap up, we verify that your oven meets our high cleanliness standards and is fully functional. Lastly, we remove all protective coverings, leaving your kitchen exactly as we found it—except with a gleaming oven ready for immediate use.
