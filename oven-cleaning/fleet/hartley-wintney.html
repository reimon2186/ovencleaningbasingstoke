Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

It all starts when you contact us for a free, no-obligation quote. After you approve our estimate, we’ll arrange a time slot that fits your calendar. On the day of your appointment, expect a brief call or message so you’ll know exactly when to expect us.

Step 2: Inspection & Safeguarding

Once we arrive, we carefully inspect your oven to identify any hidden issues and confirm all components are functioning correctly. We also protect your kitchen floors and countertops with coverings, ensuring the area remains clean and undamaged while we work.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We remove every detachable part—racks, trays, and panels—and place them in our heated dip tank in the van, which softens hardened grease. Meanwhile, we treat the oven interior with eco-friendly solutions that eliminate burnt-on grime and restore a fresh, polished look.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

After each part is thoroughly cleaned, rinsed, and dried, we polish them for a renewed shine. We then reassemble your oven carefully, ensuring every component is snug and working properly. Our precise attention to detail guarantees an oven that both looks and cooks great

Step 5: Final Touches

To wrap up, we carry out a last inspection to confirm your oven meets our strict cleanliness and performance standards. We also tidy up our workspace, leaving your kitchen exactly as it was—apart from your gleaming, rejuvenated oven, of course.
