Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

Kick things off by getting in touch with us for a free, no-obligation quote, tailored to your oven’s specific condition. Once you’re happy with our estimate, we’ll arrange an appointment that suits your schedule. On the day of service, we’ll give you a courtesy call so you know exactly when to expect us.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

Upon arrival, our technicians carry out a thorough inspection of your oven, making note of any trouble spots and confirming that all parts are functioning as they should. We also shield your kitchen floors and surfaces with protective materials, keeping the area clean and damage-free.

Step 3: Intensive Deep Clean

Next, we remove every detachable part—racks, trays, and panels—and submerge them in our specially designed heated dip tank out in the van. This process dissolves baked-on grease and carbon. Meanwhile, we treat the oven’s interior with eco-friendly solutions that effectively eliminate stubborn grime, restoring a bright, like-new finish.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

After thorough cleaning, we polish each component for a renewed shine. We then put your oven back together carefully, ensuring all pieces are properly aligned and secure. Our detail-oriented approach guarantees an oven that not only looks pristine but also runs efficiently.

Step 5: Final Touches

Before we leave, we conduct a final assessment to make sure your oven meets our high standards of cleanliness and performance. We also tidy up our workspace, leaving your kitchen exactly as we found it—except for your rejuvenated oven, ready for your next culinary adventure.
