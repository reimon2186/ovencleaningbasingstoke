Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

Reach out to us to request a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your specific requirements. Once you approve our estimate, we’ll schedule an appointment at a time that works best for you. On the day of service, expect a courtesy call letting you know our team is on the way.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

Upon arrival, we conduct a thorough inspection of your oven to identify any pre-existing issues and confirm that all components are functioning correctly. We’ll also protect your floors and kitchen counters with coverings, keeping your space pristine while we work.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We remove all detachable parts—like trays, racks, and panels—and place them in our heated dip tank located in our van. This process loosens stubborn grime. Meanwhile, we use eco-friendly solutions to remove burnt-on carbon and grease from the oven’s interior, bringing back its original shine.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

After the interior is thoroughly cleaned, we polish all surfaces to give your oven a like-new appearance. We then reassemble every component with precision, making sure everything fits securely and is working properly. Our attention to detail ensures a spotless outcome each time.

Step 5: Final Touches

Lastly, we perform a final inspection of your oven, ensuring it’s both sparkling clean and fully operational. We also tidy up our workspace, leaving your kitchen exactly as we found it—only now with a refreshed oven ready for action.
