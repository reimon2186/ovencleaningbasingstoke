Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your needs. After you approve our estimate, we’ll schedule your appointment at a convenient time. On your service day, we’ll call beforehand to ensure you’re prepared for our arrival.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

Upon arrival, our technicians perform a thorough check of your oven to spot any potential issues and verify that all components are working properly. We also protect your kitchen floors and countertops with coverings to shield them from any mess or damage during the process.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We remove all detachable parts, including racks, trays, and any panels, and place them in our heated dip tank located in the van. This approach effectively breaks down baked-on grease. At the same time, we clean your oven’s interior with eco-friendly products, eradicating burnt-on carbon and restoring its shine.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

Once the interior is fully cleaned, we polish all surfaces for a freshly renewed appearance. Then, we meticulously reassemble your oven, ensuring every part is secure and fully operational. Our diligence at this stage guarantees a spotless finish and top performance.

Step 5: Final Touches

Finally, we conduct a last inspection to confirm your oven is pristine and functioning at peak efficiency. We also tidy up our workspace, leaving your kitchen just as we found it—except now, your oven is gleaming and ready for immediate use.
