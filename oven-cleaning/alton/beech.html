Our Oven Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

Start by giving us a call or sending a message to receive a free, no-obligation quote. If you’re happy with our estimate, we’ll schedule an appointment that fits your calendar. On the day of service, we’ll give you a quick heads-up call so you’re prepared for our arrival.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

Upon arriving at your home, our technicians perform a detailed inspection of your oven to identify any existing issues and confirm that all components are working properly. We then safeguard your kitchen floors and countertops with protective materials to prevent any mess or damage during the cleaning.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

We carefully remove all detachable parts, such as racks, trays, and back panels, and place them in our heated dip tank located in the van. This process loosens stubborn grease and grime. Meanwhile, we treat the oven’s interior with eco-friendly products, dissolving baked-on carbon deposits and restoring a brilliant sheen.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

Once every interior surface is thoroughly cleaned, we polish all areas to give your oven a sleek, like-new finish. We then reassemble your oven, making sure each component is securely in place and functioning correctly. Our attention to detail ensures a spotless result every time.

Step 5: Final Touches

Finally, we carry out a comprehensive inspection of your oven, confirming that it meets our high-quality standards. We also clean up our work area, leaving your kitchen exactly as we found it—except now you have a gleaming oven ready for immediate use.
