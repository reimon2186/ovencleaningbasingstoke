Our BBQ Cleaning Process

Step 1: Booking Your Service

Start by requesting a free, no-obligation quote that reflects your BBQ’s specific size and condition. Once you approve our pricing, we’ll schedule an appointment at a time that suits you best. On the service day, anticipate a courtesy call letting you know when to expect us.

Step 2: Inspection and Preparation

Upon arrival, our technicians perform a thorough assessment of your BBQ, noting any heavily soiled or damaged parts. We then protect the surrounding patio or deck area with appropriate coverings, ensuring a clean, stress-free environment while we work.

Step 3: Deep Cleaning

Next, we remove and submerge all detachable components—such as grill plates, racks, and heat shields—in our heated dip tank. This soaking process loosens caked-on food residue and carbon deposits. Meanwhile, we treat the BBQ’s interior and exterior surfaces with eco-friendly solutions that dissolve grime and restore a polished look.

Step 4: Polishing and Reassembly

After cleaning and polishing every component, we carefully reassemble your BBQ, confirming that all parts are aligned and secured for optimal functionality. Our high standards ensure your grill not only looks fantastic but also performs flawlessly for your next gathering.

Step 5: Final Inspection & Tidy-Up

Finally, we examine your BBQ to ensure it meets our exacting standards of cleanliness and operation. We tidy up our workspace, leaving your outdoor area exactly as it was—except now you have a rejuvenated BBQ, ready to serve up memorable meals.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How often should I have my BBQ professionally cleaned?

    We recommend scheduling a professional BBQ cleaning twice a year, depending on how much you use your grill. Regular cleaning not only maintains optimal performance and hygiene but also extends the lifespan of your BBQ. We especially encourage a thorough clean before putting your BBQ away for winter. Cleaning before winter prevents rust and weathering caused by accumulated carbon and grease, ensuring your BBQ is ready to go for next summer.

  • What makes your BBQ cleaning service stand out?

    Our dedicated team is exceptionally meticulous, ensuring that every part of your BBQ—including the most hard-to-reach areas like the top surfaces and hidden corners—is cleaned to perfection. Additionally, we polish your BBQ with a special product that prevents rusting and weathering. This product forms a thin layer of oil that repels rain and prevents oxidation, protecting your BBQ from the elements and maintaining its pristine condition.

  • Do you clean all kinds of BBQs?

    Yes, we clean gas, charcoal, electric, and pellet grills. Each type requires special care, so we’ll explain what to expect from our cleaning service for each of them. This ensures that your specific grill is treated with the appropriate methods and products to achieve the best possible results.
